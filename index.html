What is Pentesting

Pentesting is made up of two words, Penetration and Testing. Let's look more into it!

Pentesting, also known as penetration testing, is a security assessment, an analysis and progression of simulated attacks. Security experts who use hacking methods to help companies identify possible entry points into their resources like web apps, mobile apps, cloud, thick apps, etc. By using different methodologies, tools and approaches, testers can perform simulated cyber attacks to test the strengths and weaknesses of their existing security systems. Penetration, in this case, refers to the degree to which a external element, or hacker, can penetrate and exploit an organization's cybersecurity measures and protocols. The scope of pentesting goes beyond merely stopping criminals from unauthorized access to a company's infrastructure. It creates real-world scenarios that show businesses how well their current defenses would fare when an exploit happens.
